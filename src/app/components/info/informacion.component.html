    POP Framework is a set of methods, techniques and tools that, combined, will provide CDOs with the appropriate choice of the data sets to be published in portals of open data. In this project, we intend to apply this Framework to the field of portals Open Data of Smart Cities. It can be considered that POP Framework is divided into 3 parts or sections:

    1. Selection and categorization of Datasets
    2. Establishment of indicators
    3. Calculation of priorities

  • Selection and categorization of Datasets

    We currently have 8960 Data Sets records extracted from GitHub, belonging to the main United States cities. To categorize it into different categories, a semantic comparison is made between the topics of each record with each of the 14 categories, so that each record will be classified in one of these categories, or in the category "Other" if it does not meet a minimum of semantic relationship with the mentioned categories.

  • Establishment of indicators

    Once the 8960 records have been categorized, a database view is created that allows the establishment of the indicators needed to perform the subsequent method (AHP method). In case of performing the Fast execution mode, these indicators are preloaded, but may not offer real information with previously categorized categories, but can offer an approximate view of the result.

  • Calculation of priorities

    To obtain the desired priorities, it is carried out in the AHP method with the indicators obtained previously. The establishment of priorities must be coherent, indicating the criterion of consistency in real time (currently established at 10%). Once the priorities are established, the AHP method is carried out and the theme is shown convenient according to the desired priorities.
